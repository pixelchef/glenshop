Company

Glenco was formed in 1997. We felt there was a gap in the testing of hydraulic components, so we set out with the goal to improve the testing equipment and procedures involved. During the journey we found that there were few services offered in this field. We also found that there was a lack of quality in equipment being used, and what was manufactured was being very poorly produced. We started building our own test equipment to meet our customers’ needs.

Due to the nature of the products and equipment we build, we are also able to offer specialized welding and fabrication services to our customers. Don’t hesitate to contact us if we can answer any other questions.
