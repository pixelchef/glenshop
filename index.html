    Hydraulic Test Systems & Equipment

    Glenco specializes in the design and manufacturing of hydraulic test equipment for the construction and mining industries. Our hydraulic test stands are dedicated diagnostic and quality control machines used to measure leakage, determine clutch pressures, check the clutch sequence and functionally test individual valves in transmission control valve packages. All our our testing systems are designed and manufactured in the USA. We also offer specialized welding and fabrication services.
